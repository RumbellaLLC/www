Rumbella was founded with the mission of addressing inefficiencies in food production and sales.

We believe that reducing food waste is a shared responsibility. By minimizing waste, we not only conserve valuable resources but also protect our environment and support those in need.

Sustainability through food rescue

A win-win situation for all

Discounts from 50% to 99%

Customers save money on groceries, food, and beverage, by using the Rumbella app and enjoy discounts starting at 50%, all the way to 99%. When you save money, you also save food.

Focus on sustainability

Rumbella collaborates with business partners to identify surplus food, as well as supplemental items to ensure this surplus finds its way to people's homes, rather than end up at the landfill. When food is saved, the environment is also saved.

Community support

Food can bring people together. Because of this, unsold food items are donated to individuals and communities in need.

Together, lets make a positive impact

We believe in a world with no food waste. By supporting Rumbella through purchases on the Rumbella app, you have the power to make a difference towards a sustainable future.

Frequently asked questions

Rumbella offers a diverse selection of food products, including meals from restaurants, fresh pastries from bakeries, and other delectable items from various local stores. All rescued food items are within their use-by date, ensuring safety and deliciousness.

At Rumbella, quality is our priority. We work closely with our partner businesses to ensure that food purchased through Rumbella meets the same high standards you will find if you bought them straight off the shelf at a physical store. On top of this, Rumbella is providing a sustainable way to enjoy tasty meals while reducing food waste.
