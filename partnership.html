Turn unsold produce into revenue with Rumbella's free‑to‑use platform

The cost of waste disposal is increasing in the UAE

Overnight, the cost of disposing of waste in landfill went up from 10 AED per visit to 100 AED per tonne of non-recyclable waste deposited.

Turn costs into revenue with Rumbella

Rumbella helps businesses identify surplus food & beverage which may be destined to become waste. Together, we can find sustainable alternatives.

Done in three easy steps

1

Send your menus and images for inclusion in your brand profile on the Rumbella marketplace.

2

Set the price, expiry date, and quantity. The system will automatically figure out when to make items available on Rumbella so full-profit, in-store sales are still maximised.

3

Customers place orders and pay directly through the Rumbella app. Orders are picked up in-person or delivered to customers using a courier organised by Rumbella. Rumbella sends the majority share of the revenue back to you.

We've got you covered

Rumbella manages, payments, delivery & customer support. You'll even have your own dedicated account manager to help you with anything you need along the way.

Go the extra mile

Any surplus items that do not find a customer can be donated to a good cause by Rumbella on behalf of our business partner free of charge. This helps individuals doing critical jobs within our society who are often malnourished.
